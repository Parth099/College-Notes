## 1.3.8

### a)

> Verify $x-1$ is a factor of $x^n - 1$

Notice that $x^n - 1 = (x-1)(x^{n-1} + x^{x-2} + \dotso + x + 1)$

### b)

$$
\begin{align}
2^{2n} \cdot 3^n -1 &= (2^2 \cdot 3)^n - 1 \\
&= 12^n - 1 \\
&= \underbrace{(12-1)}_{11}(12^{n-1} + 12^{n-2} + \dotso + 12 + 1)
\end{align}
$$

Therefore $11 | 2^{2n} \cdot 3^n -1$
